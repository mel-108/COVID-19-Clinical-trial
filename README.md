# COVID-19 Clinical Trials Analysis

## Project Overview

The COVID-19 pandemic accelerated clinical research worldwide, leading to thousands of clinical trials focused on vaccines, drugs, diagnostics, and treatment strategies. This project performs an in-depth Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) of COVID-19 clinical trial data to uncover meaningful insights into global research trends, study phases, participant demographics, and trial statuses.

The project demonstrates a complete data analysis workflow using Python, transforming raw clinical trial data into actionable insights through data cleaning, visualization, and statistical exploration.

# Objectives

- Analyze global COVID-19 clinical trial data.
- Understand the distribution of clinical trial statuses.
- Explore trends in trial phases and enrollment.
- Identify the most common study types and interventions.
- Analyze participant eligibility and age groups.
- Discover temporal trends in clinical research.
- Generate insights using data visualization.

# Key Findings

- Most COVID-19 clinical trials have reached the **Completed** status.
- The majority of studies focus on the **Adult** population.
- **Interventional** studies dominate the dataset.
- Clinical trial activity increased significantly during the pandemic.
- Most studies fall within **Phase 2** and **Phase 3**, reflecting the rapid evaluation of treatments and vaccines
